My Journey with Sorority Life

I am a sophomore in Alpha Phi at Miami University, and I absolutely love Greek life! Being a part of a sorority has not only helped me create lasting friendships but has also inspired my passion for designing and selling sorority merch.

As a Marketing and Emerging Technologies in Business and Design major, I believe that sorority merch is a convenient and stylish way to represent our sisterhood, especially in the winter months. Who doesn't love to throw on a cozy hoodie or t-shirt for class?

Additionally, I am a strong believer in uplifting women through the support and love of the Panhellenic community. My vision for this business is to not only grow but also to offer bulk orders for entire sororities, host giveaways, and create sponsorship opportunities.

Favorite Items:

  • T-Shirts: Comfortable and stylish
  • Hoodies: Perfect for chilly days
  • Accessories: Add a fun touch to any outfit
